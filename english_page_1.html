Tantalum!

Element name: tantalum (Ta)

Atomic mass: 180.94788 u

Atomic number: 73

Isotopes number: 2

Mass number (isotopes): 180Ta and 181Ta

Melting temperature: 3,017 °C

Boiling temperature: 5,458 °C

Density: 16.654 g/cm³

Electronegativity: 1.5 on Pauling's scale

Year of descovery: 1802

Abundance: It is found primarily in the minerals tantalite and holovotantalite, and its abundance in the Earth's crust is about 1 ppm.

Characteristics: It is a transition metal known for its resistance to corrosion and its high melting point. It is a dark gray, shiny metal.
