The Cerium(Ce)

Characteristic:

Cerium is a silvery-white, soft, and fairly reactive metal. It is easily oxidized in air, forming an oxide patina on the surface.Is the most abundant lanthanide in the Earth's crust.

Major producing countries with large reserves are:

  • China
  • Brazil
  • India
  • Russia
  • United States
  • Vietnam
  • Furthermore, it can remain stable for a long time in the environment, especially in industrial sludge.

  • Boiling point: 3,230 °C
  • Density: 6.77 g/cm³
  • Electronegativity: 1.12 (Pauling scale)
  • ear of discovery: 1803
